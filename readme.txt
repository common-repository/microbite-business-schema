== Description ==

The Microbite Business Schema Plugin is a transformative tool designed specifically for local businesses aiming to solidify their online presence and enhance their visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs).

This innovative plugin simplifies the complex process of integrating structured data, or schema markup, into your website, making it more intelligible to search engines like Google, Bing, and Yahoo.

Structured data is a standardized format for providing information about a page and classifying the page content.

For local businesses, this means being able to highlight essential information such as your business type, location, hours of operation, contact information, and more, directly within search results.

By implementing local business schema, the Microbite Plugin makes your website not just a source of information but a standout entity that search engines can easily understand and prioritize in local search queries.

The core feature of the Microbite Business Schema Plugin is its user-friendly interface, which includes a straightforward dropdown menu for selecting your precise business type.

This feature eliminates the guesswork and technical hurdles often associated with schema markup, allowing you to customize your site's data without needing extensive SEO knowledge or development skills.

Whether you're a small shop, a service provider, or a mid-sized company, the plugin caters to a wide array of local business types, ensuring that your business gets the spotlight it deserves.

In addition to enhancing your SEO, the Microbite Business Schema Plugin serves as a beacon for search engines, guiding them through your site's most important content and helping your business rank higher for relevant searches.

This targeted approach not only improves your search engine rankings but also increases the likelihood of your business being featured in rich snippets and knowledge graphs, which are highly coveted positions on the SERPs.

Moreover, the plugin is continually updated to reflect the latest in SEO best practices and schema standards, ensuring that your business remains at the cutting edge of digital marketing.

With its blend of simplicity, effectiveness, and a focus on local businesses, the Microbite Business Schema Plugin is an indispensable asset for any local business looking to enhance its online presence, attract more customers, and compete more effectively in the digital marketplace.

Check out the [Plugin Homepage](https://www.microbite.com.au/microbite-business-schema/) for more info.



== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `microbie-business-schema` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. DO NOT change the name of the `microbie-business-schema` folder.
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
5. Look at Microbite Business Schema on the dashboard to see instructions on how to use.

= What local business are supported? =


The Microbite Business Schema Plugin supports a wide range of local business and organization types. Here are some of the categories you can select from:

General Business: Local Business, Animal Shelter, Library, Real-Estate Agent, Travel Agency, etc.
Automotive Business: Auto Repair, Auto Dealer, Motorcycle Repair, Auto Parts Store, etc.
Emergency Services: Hospital, Fire Station, Police Station.
Entertainment Business: Art Gallery, Casino, Comedy Club, Movie Theater, Night Club, etc.
Financial Service: Bank or Credit Union, Accounting Service, Insurance Agency.
Food Establishment: Bakery, Brewery, Restaurant, Cafe or Coffee Shop, Fast-Food Restaurant, etc.
Health and Beauty Business: Beauty Salon, Health Club, Nail Salon, Tattoo Parlor.
Home and Construction Business: Electrician, General Contractor, Locksmith, Moving Company, Plumber, etc.
Legal Service: Attorney, Notary.
Lodging Business: Hotel, Resort, Motel, Bed and Breakfast, Hostel, etc.
Medical Business: Dentist, Hospital, Medical Clinic, Physiotherapy, Psychiatry, etc.
Sports Activity Location: Golf Course, Health Club, Stadium or Arena, Tennis Complex, etc.
Store: Book Store, Clothing Store, Electronics Store, Grocery Store, Pet Store, etc.
